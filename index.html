Sin Sze Man

Theater and Music Performer, Writer and Psychologist

I was born in Hong Kong, I grew up with an interesting family dynamic. But I was exposed to music at a very young age, I had piano lessons weekly, and around twelve to thirteen I also started playing guitar. I came to Massachusetts for a high school education. I went to a boarding school the summer I turned fourteen, and since then became even more independent than I already was.

I was excited with the opportunity to get to explore the many artistic projects. I get to expand my knowledge and express my love for theatre, film, song-writing, screen-writing, languages and more. I graduated at seventeen and decide to explore the Southern California lifestyle, mainly Los Angeles. So I applied to the University of California. My goal as an undergrad at University of California, Riverside is pursuing a double major in psychology and in acting.

During the most recent winter break, I focused on dance, in the genre of jazz funk, at a dance studio in Hong Kong. In other times during this break, I’ve enjoyed going to museums and educating myself with more artistic perspectives. Upcoming summer, I am planning to join a research based program to understand better the field of psychology and the deeper depth of child development in a global perspective.
